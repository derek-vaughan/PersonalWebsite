The Impossible List.

"Sort of like an iterative bucket list"

I've put together and published this list to accomplish two things for myself: 1. Keep myself accountable, and 2. Ensure I am always working towards "the next cool thing". As I mentioned on the hompage of this website, I'm someone who can easily waste away days, weeks, or even months if I don't have anything to work towards - it's happened before, and I'm sure that it could happen again. The goal of this list is to ensure I never have to look back on any period of time, and think "I haven't really been doing much..."

This list was created on February 6th, 2021, and is meant to be treated sort of like an iterative bucket list, where the goals may start small, but I aim to increase the scope of them whenever they are accomplished. Some goals on here are retroactively filled in, but many of them have 2/6/2021 as a "Day #1" starting point.

Finally, I may add or remove goals as my life's priorities shift - but the overarching goal of this list will always remain the same.
